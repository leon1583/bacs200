The Hero Who Inspires Caleb:

William Borden

What would it look like for a rich young ruler to sell all of his possessions and give it to the poor, and choose to follow Jesus? William Borden has this story.

William Borden was born to a very welalthy family in Chicago. After graduating from high school, William Borden attended Yale. While in college, he founded the non-profit organization Yale Resuce Mission where he helped feed the homeless. After college, Borden felt a strong call to go to China as a missionary to Chinese Muslims. Forsaking his inhertience, he spent 6 months in Egypt learning Arabic until he passed away from a disease unexpectedly. He was just 25 years old, yet his legacy rippled the world.

After his death, his family found written in his Bible: "No Reserves. No Retreats. No Regrets. I hope that my life story will have no reserves in loving God, no retreats from loving others and no regrets for giving it my all for Him.
